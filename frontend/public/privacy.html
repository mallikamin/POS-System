Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our POS System ("we", "our", "the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our restaurant point-of-sale and accounting integration software.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate the POS system, including: user account credentials (encrypted), order transaction data, restaurant configuration, and QuickBooks Online accounting data that you authorize us to access via OAuth 2.0.

3. QuickBooks Online Data

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we access your Chart of Accounts, Items, Tax Codes, and create Sales Receipts on your behalf. We only access data necessary to synchronize POS transactions with your accounting system. Your QuickBooks OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (Fernet/AES).

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to: operate the POS system, synchronize transactions with QuickBooks Online, generate business reports, and provide customer support. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including: HTTPS encryption in transit, encrypted credential storage, JWT-based authentication, and role-based access control. Database access is restricted and all sensitive tokens are encrypted at rest.

6. Data Retention

We retain transaction and accounting data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You may disconnect your QuickBooks Online account at any time through the application settings. Upon disconnection, we cease accessing your QuickBooks data. You may request a copy or deletion of your data at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the application.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the application support channel.
