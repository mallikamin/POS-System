Notes & Disclaimers

  1. Net Revenue is defined as gross SaaS subscription payments minus direct hosting costs, payment gateway fees, third-party API/license costs, and applicable taxes/statutory deductions (MOU Section 4.2).
  2. IP Ownership: All intellectual property, source code, architecture, and deployment systems remain the exclusive property of Sitara Infotech. BPO World receives a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to market and sell (MOU Section 3).
  3. Custom Development (new verticals, bespoke modules, client-specific integrations) is scoped separately via SOW and is 100% payable to Sitara Infotech unless expressly agreed otherwise in the SOW (MOU Section 4.5).
  4. Feature Commitments: BPO World shall not make any representations, warranties, or commitments to clients regarding the Platform's features or capabilities that have not been authorised in writing by Sitara Infotech (MOU Section 13.3c).
  5. Revenue share is lifetime for each BPO World-sourced client, for the duration of the MOU. Upon termination, a 12-month transition period applies with continued revenue share on existing clients (MOU Sections 4.7 and 11).
  6. Pricing shown is a baseline for Pakistan-market restaurant clients. International clients (e.g., Kuwait) and new verticals (e.g., petrol pump) may require adjusted pricing via separate SOW.
  7. Deductions are calculated on actual costs attributable to the specific client's usage, not averaged or estimated. Sitara Infotech provides a detailed monthly statement for each client (MOU Schedule B, Note b).
  8. Non-compete: BPO World agrees to a 2-year non-compete post-termination covering competing POS/SaaS platforms, with non-solicitation of Sitara Infotech personnel (MOU Section 9).
  9. Confidentiality survives 3 years post-termination. Covers pricing structures, client lists, and technical architecture (MOU Section 8).
  10. This document is a summary for discussion purposes. The binding terms are set out in the full MOU (Document Ref: SV-BPO/MOU/2026-001).
